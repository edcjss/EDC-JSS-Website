Financial Literacy Club
The club was founded in January 2021. to provide young adults with the necessary personal finance knowledge, to comprehend how business grows, and learn money investing skills in an interactive setting. The root idea is to make our peers understand the psychology of money and ways in which we can apply financial wisdom to grow our money. Though in our nascent stage, at FLC, our goal is to encourage young people to put in cognizant efforts towards making informed financial decisions from the beginning to have a more secure financial future and to avoid the common financial pitfalls that many adults face.

Women Empowerment Club
Ojaswini is the name we gave to the social drive launched by EDC that solely targets the local women doing small business on their own. The drive was brought to the mainstage with the help of "Women Entrepreneurship and Empowerment(WEE) Foundation". We reach out to women owning small businesses and extend our help by getting them enrolled in government schemes and provide guidance by professionals and making them aware of their rights as a citizen of our nation. This event is organized to celebrate the essence of woman entrepreneurs in every field and endorse the spirit of women empowerment among the youth in attendance.
